About


Deaf Online (DOL) Co.


This company was created to help the Deaf Community connect with each other exclusively on an application built just for them. Deaf Online (DOL) is built with a simple design but instead focuses on functionality. Our company features lots of useful components to connect including a blog, weekly newsletter, and traveling wishlist to meet up in person. We are committed to serving the Deaf Community as a whole.
